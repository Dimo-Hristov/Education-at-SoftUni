The Brown Bear

The brown bear (Ursus arctos) is a large bear species found across Eurasia and North America. In North
America, the populations of brown bears are called grizzly bears, while the subspecies that inhabits the
Kodiak Islands of Alaska is known as the Kodiak bear.
